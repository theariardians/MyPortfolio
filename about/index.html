About Me

I am a passionate UI/UX Designer and Graphic Designer who strives to create attractive and functional design experiences. Currently, I am pursuing a degree in Computer Science at the Muhammadiyah University of Bangka Belitung. Throughout my journey as a designer, I have honed my expertise in designing intuitive user interfaces, conducting user research, prototyping, and performing UX analysis. I firmly believe that good design emerges from the amalgamation of technical acumen and a profound understanding of user requirements.

I relish the challenge of blending visually appealing aesthetics with efficient functionality in every design project I undertake. Collaborating with diverse, interdisciplinary teams, exchanging ideas, and discovering optimal solutions to accomplish design objectives bring me immense joy. Additionally, I constantly endeavor to enhance my skills by staying abreast of the latest advancements in the design industry. I actively participate in seminars, devour books, and enroll in online courses to remain up-to-date with contemporary design trends and innovative tools.

Should you be interested in collaborating on a design project or sharing design knowledge, please do not hesitate to reach out to me. I am thrilled to establish new connections within the realm of design.
